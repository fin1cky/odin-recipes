Shakshouka

Description

Easy and delicious eggs poached in a spicy tomato sauce. A popular Middle Eastern dish that has become a favorite with many families. Don't forget flatbreads or crusty bread for dipping!

This recipe needs just 20 mins of Prep Time and 15 mins of Cook Time! Start your day with a healthy and tasty Shakshouka breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, halved and finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red chile pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a heavy-bottomed frying pan.
  2. Saute onion and bell peppers in the hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Add chile pepper and garlic; cook and stir the peppers start to caramelize, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and sugar. Cook over medium heat for 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Make indentations in the sauce using a wooden spoon. Crack eggs into indentations in the sauce. Cover and cook until whites are firm and yolks reach desired doneness, 5 to 8 minutes.
  7. Uncover and garnish with green onions.
